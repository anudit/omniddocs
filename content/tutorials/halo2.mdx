## Setup the Project

Fork the template, https://github.com/anudit/halo2-template.
It simplifies the process of compiling Halo2 circuits in a highly customizable way.

Your project structure should look something like this.

<Files>
  <Folder name="build"/>
  <Folder name="circomlib"/>
  <Folder name="circuits" defaultOpen>
    <File name="CheckMult.circom" />
  </Folder>
  <Folder name="scripts"/>
  <Folder name="templates"/>
  <Folder name="tests" defaultOpen>
   <File name="checkMult.test.ts" />
  </Folder>
  <File name="package.json" />
</Files>
